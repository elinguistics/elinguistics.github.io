This is the website of the eLinguistics Foundation (eLF), the parent organization of Linguist List. The eLF, as part of its mission to encourage the construction of infrastructure for the discipline of linguistics and to provide financial aid to such efforts, announces a request for proposals for Linguistic Fieldwork Support Grants.

Linguistic Fieldwork Support Grants

Proposals for grants in support of linguistic fieldwork should include:

  1. Project title
  2. Name of country, region, and languages to be studied and/or documented
  3. Description of proposed work (not to exceed # pages). The description should include goals of the project, significance/impact of the project for linguistics and/or the population studied, anticipated outcomes/products, and a timeline of work.
  4. References
  5. Budget and budget justification
  6. Biosketch
  7. Other current and pending support for the project
  8. Starting and ending dates

Projects may be 6 to 18 months in duration. Proposals may be for up to $6000. Application deadline: 10 July 2024. Applications should be sent to treasurer@linguistlist.org by July 10, 2024.
